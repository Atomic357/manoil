About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence

Proudly Indigenous Engineering and Energy Solutions

Manoli Projects Ltd is a proudly indigenous engineering and energy solutions company incorporated in Nigeria in 2019. Founded by a team of seasoned professionals with a shared history of successful collaboration, we are driven by a bold vision.

We specialize in providing total renewable energy solutions—with a strong emphasis on solar power technologies—as well as comprehensive MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) services, civil engineering works, and technical manufacturing consultancy and training.

Our full-service approach is backed by a team of deeply experienced engineers, project managers, and technical experts who are committed to delivering quality solutions—on time, within budget, and tailored to each client's unique needs.

Quality, Health, Safety & Environment

Our commitment to zero-accident workplace and environmental responsibility

Toolbox Meetings & Safety Training

We provide daily 20-minute 'toolbox' training sessions for all employees and weekly safety-training sessions on topics of current concern. Our site-specific hazard assessments ensure relevant and effective safety programs.

Leadership and Commitment

Our supervisors are responsible for Safety, Health and Environment, reinforcing Manoil policies through quarterly roundtable meetings with employees. Serious incidents involve immediate notification of management.

Implementation and Monitoring

We establish performance measures and hold all levels of management accountable. Our root-cause investigation philosophy applies to incidents involving lost-time, fatalities, or significant injuries.

Risk Management

We evaluate each work location using thorough Hazard Assessments that define hazard-abatement procedures. Regular site-safety inspections and internal audits ensure compliance with our HSE Management System.
